Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2026

HabitUnlock ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Health Data (with your permission)

With your explicit consent, we read the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Step Count — to track walking habits
  • Exercise Minutes — to track workout habits
  • Stand Hours — to track movement habits
  • Workout Sessions — to track completed exercises

We do NOT write any data to HealthKit. We only read data to determine if your habits are complete.

Local Data (stored on your device only)

  • Your custom habits and goals
  • Streak history and achievements
  • App preferences and settings
  • Anonymous analytics events (never uploaded to any server)

Data Storage

All your data stays on your device. HabitUnlock is designed as an offline-first app. We do not have servers that store your personal information.

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or transmit your personal data to any third parties. Your health data, habits, and usage patterns remain entirely on your device.

Screen Time & App Blocking

HabitUnlock uses Apple's Screen Time API (Family Controls) to block apps until habits are complete. This data is processed locally by Apple's framework and is never accessible to us.

Analytics

We collect anonymous usage analytics (such as which features are used) stored locally on your device. These analytics are never uploaded to any external service. You can disable analytics in the app settings.

Third-Party Services

HabitUnlock uses the following Apple services:

  • Apple HealthKit — to read health data (with your permission)
  • Apple Screen Time API — to block apps (processed locally)
  • App Store / StoreKit — to process subscriptions (handled by Apple)

Children's Privacy

HabitUnlock is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."
